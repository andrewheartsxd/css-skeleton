About Us

Champion Congee began as a side project when Andrew realized he couldn't get hot, wholesome congee on par with what his mom made while working in Seattle as a software developer. After obtaining funding from backers including Gordon Ramsay, Jiro Ono, and Elon Musk, Champion Congee opened at its first location on Amazon's SLU campus serving congee crafted from the finest organic, locally sourced ingredients. Following critical acclaim, the Champion Congee truck now caters to a fanatic following around the globe.
